Abramba Technologies

A company focused on the design and manufacturing of solar and efficient lighting products for the residential, commercial and public sectors in Ethiopia. We aspire to be one of the leading technology pioneers positioned to support and sustain the capacity building of Ethiopia’s nascent efficient lighting and renewable energy industry.

Our Vision

Be the leading provider of lighting and optimal power solutions to the African market.

Our Mission

Help eliminate energy poverty and improve the standard of living of the people of Africa to achieve economic success.

What We Do

Abramba Technologies is a company focused on the business of designing, manufacturing and marketing high quality solar and efficient lighting technology products in Africa. Our products and solutions have practical applications in providing lighting and electrical power in the residential, commercial and public domains. 

Our Expertise

Our team brings a broad range of knowledge and business management experience in many fields including solar systems and power engineering, marketing, manufacturing, finance, legal and regulatory practices. Our partners bring rich expertise in solar energy technologies, products and applications development, offering our customers a broad range of products and customized solar power systems solutions.

Our Parent Company

Newport Spectrum Technologies (NST) is the holding company of Abramba Technologies. NST, headquartered in Irvine, California, and its founders bring extensive managerial, marketing, technological and manufacturing expertise based on decades of experience in the global high-tech industry. Leveraging the use of its subsidiary – Abramba Technologies – NST will operate a solar and efficient energy R&D and manufacturing facility in Ethiopia. NST will also pursue other related technology collaborations and synergistic partnerships in the larger global community to address and participate in expanding business opportunities.
